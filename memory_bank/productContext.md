# Product Context

## Why This Product Exists
Recipe Finder should help users quickly find suitable dishes either from a known recipe name or from ingredients they already have. The product is aimed at reducing decision fatigue and making cooking discovery feel playful instead of utilitarian.

## Target Users
- Young adults aged 14-30.
- Users who want a lightweight cooking assistant on mobile and desktop.
- Users who value visual engagement and simple search flows.

## User Problems
- Users do not always know what meal to cook from available ingredients.
- Generic recipe sites can feel overloaded and visually dense.
- New cooks need a fast path from search intent to recipe details.

## Product Expectations
- Fast search flow.
- Friendly visual style.
- Responsive interface.
- Eventual real recipe data and detailed recipe pages.
